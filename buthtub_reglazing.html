What is Bathtub Reglazing?

Bathtub reglazing, also known as bathtub refinishing or resurfacing, is a process that involves restoring and rejuvenating the surface of your bathtub. Over time, bathtubs can become dull, stained, scratched, or outdated. Instead of going through the hassle and expense of a full bathtub replacement, reglazing offers a cost-effective and efficient solution.
